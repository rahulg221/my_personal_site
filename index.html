About Me

Hi, I'm Rahul. I am a second year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Central Florida. I am determined to make a meaningful difference and I'm passionate about innovating solutions to real-world problems with the use of technology. I began my journey as a kid making websites in WordPress and platformer games in Scratch. In High School I took courses in Web Development and Java foundations, and I became MTA-certified in HTML, CSS, and Java. Now I am attending the University of Central Florida, where I became proficient in the C language and took courses in Data Structures and Algorithms, Security in Computing, and Computer Logic and Organization. Lately, I have been invested in learning mobile development, and I have been developing my own applications using the Flutter framework and Dart language. I am excited to keep learning, and I have big plans for the future.

Outside of school and programming, I am an avid musician, gym-goer, and martial arts student. I enjoy playing the piano and cello, lifting weights, and attending UCF's Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu club. I also spend my free time watching Bollywood movies and Anime, and hanging out with my family, friends, and girlfriend.
